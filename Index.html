The Batman

"The dark knight"

Batman is a legendary superhero from DC Comics, known as the Dark Knight and the protector of Gotham City. Unlike many heroes, he has no superpowers; instead,
he relies on his intelligence, detective skills, physical training, and advanced technology to fight crime. After witnessing the murder of his parents as a child, Bruce Wayne dedicates his life to justice, using his wealth to create the Batman identity. He operates from the Batcave, drives the iconic Batmobile, and faces some of the most famous villains in comic history, including the Joker, Catwoman, and the Penguin.
